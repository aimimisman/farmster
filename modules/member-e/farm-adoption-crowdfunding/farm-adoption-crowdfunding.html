Trust and safety FAQ

Clear answers reduce hesitation and make the adoption flow feel safer.

How are growers verified?

Growers submit identity, farm location, project details, and supporting photos before a project is approved.

How are funds released?

Funds can be released based on milestones such as material purchase, planting, and harvest reporting.

What happens if a project is not funded?

Supporters can be refunded or allowed to redirect funds to another verified project, depending on platform rules.

Will supporters receive updates?

Yes. Growers can post photo updates, milestone notes, and final impact reports through the dashboard.

Are you a grower looking for support?

Create a verified project page, explain your funding needs, and connect with supporters who care about local food production.
