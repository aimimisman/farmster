How It Works

Harvested today,
delivered tomorrow

We've built a seamless system that respects the farmer's cycle and the buyer's time. Simple, transparent, and deeply human.

01
Browse & Discover

Explore hundreds of fresh listings directly from certified Malaysian farms. Filter by region, crop, and harvest date.

02
Find Local Farmers

Locate nearby farmers and see exactly where and when they are selling their harvest in your community. Track market locations and setup times easily so you can grab your produce fresh at the source.

03
Meet & Buy Directly

Meet face-to-face with community farmers to buy your produce directly from the hands that grew it. Skip retail markups and grocery delays, ensuring fair prices for you and honest earnings for them.

04
Eat & Reconnect

Enjoy honest food knowing exactly where it came from. Rate your experience and build a relationship with your farmer.
