Project Overview

What is this project about?

This platform supports local farmers and makes it easier for consumers to discover farm products online.

The Farm Marketplace Platform allows users to view available farm products, check product information, compare market prices, and contact producers directly.

It also supports trust and transparency by showing farmer profiles, farm locations, product labels, and certifications such as Organic or Pesticide-Free.

To make the project easier to manage, the system is divided into module groups. Each group is assigned to one team member, and each member can place one combined link for all their modules.

Platform Features

Main Features

The platform focuses on product discovery, trust, communication, support, and payment flow.

Browse Products

Users can view farm products and check details such as name, category, quantity, and stock.

Compare Prices

Users can compare producer prices with current market prices before making a decision.

Search and Filter

Users can search products based on type, label, location, and selected criteria.

Chat with Farmers

Consumers and producers can communicate through the chat or messaging module.

Support Farms

Users can support farms through the Farm Adoption or Crowdfunding feature.

Payment Updates

The platform supports Stripe payment links and email notifications for important updates.
